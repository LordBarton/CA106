Benito Mussolini

Benito Mussolini was an Italian politician and the leader of the National Fascist Party. Mussolini ruled Italy as Prime Minister from 1922 to 1943. He played a significant role in the rise of fascism in Europe during the early 20th century.

Fascism is a political ideology that promotes authoritarian rule and extreme nationalism. Mussolini's rule was characterized by his dictatorial control, suppression of political opposition, and aggressive foreign policies. However, his alliance with Nazi Germany during World War II ultimately led to his downfall.
